Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal, a great healthy breakfast with a balanced macro profile.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Pour hot water into a pot
  2. Heat the pot until the water is boiling
  3. Put the eggs in the pot and wait 5 minutes
  4. Remove the pot from the heat and get rid of the water after it calms down
  5. Peel the eggshell off the eggs
  6. Eat!
